## **Forensic Science at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences**
### Graduation product (year 4)
_Time period:_ 02/2025 to 07/2025<br/>
_Commissioning company:_ Netherlands Forensic Institute
<br/>
<br/>
_Description:_ This internship consisted of work on two different topics:
* Profieciency test: Creating a feedback report according to the 'framework of the conduct of proficiency tests within ENFSI': containing the variety in results, methods and conclusions of different accredited organizations for determining the provenance of images from smartphones released past the conduct of the last proficiency test.
* Experiment: Determining the influence of 'night mode'/'night sight' on the PRNU-pattern in images obtained from smartphones released past the year 2018

<br/>

_Accomplishments:_
* Written a Python Script which can indicate the origin of images based on a folder containing .csv files with cross-correlationvalues of the compared images.
* Performed a proficiency test to control the competence of accredited (forensic) organisations by examining the variance in results and conclusions, when determining  the origin of images acquired from smartphones by using different (PRNU-based) camera identity determination methods.
* Obtaining and reading metadata through exiftool and ffmpeg.
* Written multiple smaller scripts for Command Prompt in Wondows:
  * Stripping all metadata
  * Adjusting resolution
  * Rotating images
  * Flipping images
